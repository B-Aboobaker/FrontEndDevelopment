Welcome to the official PUBG website

PLAYERUNKNOWN'S BATTLEGROUNDS or more commonly called 'PUBG' is a multi-verse franchise that branches from competitive survival shooters (PUBG) to survival horror (The Callisto Protocol) video game titles. The base lore of PUBG is based around a fictional island called 'Erangel' where citizens take part in a Battle Royale, 1 vs 100 or squad vs squad.

Available on Computer

You can download, install and play PUBG on your computer from various places like Steam.

Available on PlayStore

PUBG Mobile is also available on PlayStore if you like to play on the go.
